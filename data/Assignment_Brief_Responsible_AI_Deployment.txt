Assignment Brief - Generative AI Case Study

Overview
You will work in a group of 5 to research, analyse, and present a recent real-world case or incident involving a Generative AI-powered system.

Your chosen case may cover these issues:
- Ethical considerations
- Risk mitigation and management
- Governance and compliance

This project makes up 20% of your total course grade. Deadline is on 22 May 2026, 1159pm.

Your Task

1. Select a Recent GenAI Case Study
- Pick a real-world incident from news, research, or industry reports.
- Explain how and why you chose it.
- Link it clearly to concepts and principles from our course.

2. Analyse the Case
- Discuss what happened, why it happened, and its implications.
- Evaluate the impact: on the organisation, society, economy, and environment.
- Investigate the root cause(s) behind the incident.
- Examine the response from the organisation/agency involved:
  - Was it resolved? How?
  - Was the remediation effective in preventing future issues?
  - If not, propose a better strategy, explaining why it could work.

3. Slides
- Submit a deck of slides about your case study. There should be 8-10 slides, excluding title slide and content slide if any, and should reflect the details discussed above. Submit via the NTUlearn portal.

Assessment Criteria
Your assignment will be graded as follows:

1. Case Description - 20%
What We're Looking For:
Clear, detailed description; strong rationale for case selection; strong connection to course concepts.

2. Impact Analysis - 25%
What We're Looking For:
Depth and breadth of analysis on the magnitude and severity of the incident's impact across multiple domains.

3. Root Cause Analysis - 25%
What We're Looking For:
Insightful, thorough explanation of why the issues occurred.

4. Organisational Response or Resolution - 20%
What We're Looking For:
Detailed evaluation of the response; effectiveness of solutions; thoughtful alternative strategies.

5. Presentation Structure - 10%
What We're Looking For:
Organised, engaging, well-structured, with clear communication and insightful conclusions.
